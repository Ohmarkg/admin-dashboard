The 75 / 20 / 5 Balance

Primary — 75% Secondary — 20%

To introduce the university, lean hard on maroon & white (75%), balance with neutral secondaries (20%), and reserve accents for the final 5%.

Voice & Tone

We sound confident, warm and service-minded. Plain verbs, active voice, a little Aggie pride — never corporate, never cold.

This is what it means to be an Aggie: to stand together as a force for good. — Brand Narrative
  • ProudLead with character and tradition, from the Corps to the 12th Man.
  • WarmStart with “Howdy.” Speak to the Aggie Family like family.
  • PurposefulTie every message to service — for Texas, the nation and beyond.
  • ClearActive voice, concrete nouns, no filler. Say the thing.
